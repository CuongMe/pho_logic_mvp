Privacy Policy — Phở Logic

Last updated: 2026-01-28

1) Overview

Phở Logic (“the App”) is an indie-developed mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected and used when you use the App.

2) No Account Required

  • The App does not require an account.
  • The App does not ask you to enter your name, phone number, address, or other profile details.

3) Information the App Does Not Collect Directly

The developer does not run their own servers to collect user accounts or user profiles. However, the App uses Google services (listed below) that may automatically collect certain information to provide advertising and analytics functionality.

4) Google Services Used (Third Parties)

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (Rewarded Ads)
  • Firebase Analytics

5) Information That May Be Collected Automatically (by Google Services)

Depending on your device, settings, and how the App is used, Google services may collect information such as:

  • Device and app information (e.g., device model, operating system version, language, app version)
  • App activity/usage data (e.g., events such as session starts, feature interactions, screens viewed)
  • Diagnostics/performance data (e.g., crash information, performance metrics)
  • Advertising information for rewarded ads (e.g., ad impressions, clicks, reward events)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID) where available/allowed by your settings

The specific data collected can vary based on your device, Android/Google settings, and Google service behavior.

6) How This Information May Be Used

  • To deliver rewarded ads and measure ad performance (AdMob)
  • To understand app usage and improve gameplay and features (Firebase Analytics)
  • To maintain core functionality on Android devices (Google Play Services)

7) Sharing of Information

The developer does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by the Google services listed above to provide ads and analytics features.

8) Your Choices

  • Ad personalization: You can limit ad personalization and reset/limit advertising identifiers in your Android/Google settings (options vary by device and Android version).
  • Stop collection: You can uninstall the App at any time to stop further data collection by the App and its SDKs.

9) Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you are a parent/guardian and believe data has been collected inappropriately from children, please contact google play store for support.

10) Data Retention

The developer does not store user accounts. Retention of analytics/advertising data is handled by Google services and may depend on their settings and policies.

11) Security

I take reasonable steps to protect information and to keep the App up to date. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

The App does not operate its own user account system or private database. Advertising and analytics functions are provided through Google services (such as Google Play Services, Google AdMob, and Firebase Analytics). Data processed by these services is protected according to their security practices, policies, and settings.

12) Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

13) Feedback (Google Forms)

I offer an optional feedback form to help improve the App. Providing feedback is completely optional and not required to use the App.

The feedback form is hosted by Google Forms, a third-party service operated by Google. When you submit feedback through this form:

  • I do not collect email addresses through the form.
  • I do not require you to sign in or authenticate to submit feedback.
  • The only data collected is what you voluntarily type into the form fields (such as your comments, suggestions, or optional demographic information if present).

Important: Please do not include sensitive personal information (such as financial data, passwords, government IDs, or health information) in your feedback. The form is intended for general comments and suggestions about the App only.

Google Forms is subject to Google's own privacy policies and terms of service. By submitting feedback, you acknowledge that your responses may be processed by Google in accordance with their policies.

14) Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the Developer contact email through Google Play Store listing for this app.
